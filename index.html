Learn About Me

Currently, I am working at the TUM (Technische Universität München) and developing new visualization techniques for medical augmented reality applications. They focus on improving depth and shape perception for medical doctors during a minimal invasive surgery. See below some images of state of the art visualization methods. Our work adds new dynamic visualization cues to further enhance perception. In a study, we want to evaluate the effect.

Fresnel Effect

Frosted Effect
